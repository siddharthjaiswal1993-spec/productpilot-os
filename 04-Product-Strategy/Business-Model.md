# Business Model

## Business Model Philosophy

ProductPilot OS is priced to land with PM teams and expand with the product organization. The model is SaaS seat-based with usage tiers — low friction to start, high value at scale. Every pricing decision is made to optimize for: (1) fast time to first value, (2) clear expansion trigger at team scale, (3) enterprise commercial terms that satisfy procurement requirements.

We do not do freemium. We do a 14-day free trial with full feature access. Freemium in enterprise SaaS creates a large inactive free user base that consumes infrastructure without producing revenue or feedback signal. A free trial converts only teams that experienced enough value in 14 days to commit — a higher-quality signal.

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Starter — $49/PM/month (billed annually)

**For:** Small PM teams (2–5 PMs), Series A–B companies, or design partner phase

**Includes:**
- Up to 5 PM seats
- 3 data source integrations (Slack + Jira + one more)
- 1,000 signals ingested per month
- Signal Intelligence Hub
- Opportunity Brief Generator (10 briefs/PM/month)
- PRD Copilot (5 PRDs/month)
- Basic confidence scoring
- Email support

**What's excluded:**
- Unlimited signal ingestion
- Knowledge graph
- Stakeholder Alignment Center
- Risk Watchdog
- Advanced agent orchestration
- API access
- SSO / SAML

**Expansion trigger:** Team exceeds 5 PMs, or brief/PRD volume hits tier ceiling, or needs knowledge graph for institutional memory.

---

### Tier 2: Team — $99/PM/month (billed annually)

**For:** Growth-stage PM teams (5–25 PMs), Series B–C companies

**Includes:**
- Up to 25 PM seats
- All integrations (all 14 data sources)
- Unlimited signal ingestion
- All 8 agents (full feature access)
- Product Knowledge Graph
- Meeting Intelligence Agent
- Stakeholder Alignment Center
- Risk Watchdog
- Advanced confidence scoring with component breakdown
- Priority support (4-hour response SLA)
- Slack workspace integration for support

**Expansion trigger:** Team exceeds 25 PMs, or needs SSO, RBAC, audit logs (Enterprise gates), or enterprise SLA.

---

### Tier 3: Enterprise — Custom pricing

**For:** Enterprise product organizations, 25+ PMs, Fortune 500, or regulated industries

**Includes everything in Team, plus:**
- Unlimited PM seats
- SAML 2.0 / SSO
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Audit logs (2-year retention, exportable)
- Data residency options (US, EU, APAC)
- Custom data retention policies
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Quarterly business reviews
- Custom integration development (SLA)
- BAA for HIPAA compliance (if needed)
- Enterprise MSA and DPA
- SLA: 99.9% uptime, 2-hour critical incident response

**Typical pricing:** $150–$250/PM/month for 25–100 PMs. Negotiated downward at 100+ seats.

Beyond seat counts, usage-based pricing applies to:

| Usage Dimension | Tier 1 Limit | Tier 2 Limit | Enterprise |
|----------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Signals ingested/month | 1,000 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Briefs generated/month | 10/PM | Unlimited | Unlimited |

| Knowledge graph entities | N/A | 10,000 | Unlimited |
| Meeting transcripts/month | N/A | 50 | Unlimited |

## Land and Expand Motion

The commercial architecture is designed for a specific expansion path:

**Land:** Close with 2–5 PM seats at Starter or Team tier. The champion is typically a Senior PM or Head of PM who has experienced signal collapse personally and wants to solve it for their team.

**Expand trigger 1:** PM team grows — natural seat expansion at $49–$99/PM.

**Expand trigger 2:** VP Product or CPO sees the evidence-backing rate dashboard and wants org-wide visibility — upgrade to Team or Enterprise.

**Expand trigger 3:** CS, Sales, or Engineering teams want to access intelligence or provide signals — additional seat types at different price points (Year 2 capability).

**Expand trigger 4:** Knowledge graph value is demonstrated — team is unwilling to lose the organizational memory, creating contract lock-in without contractual obligation.

Target NRR (Year 2): >120%. Expansion accounts for >40% of incremental ARR.

## Enterprise Commercial Terms

Enterprise procurement requires specific contract elements. ProductPilot OS is built to clear these gates from Day 1:

- **BAA:** Available for HIPAA-covered entities
- **DPA:** Standard Data Processing Agreement available for GDPR compliance
- **Security questionnaire:** SOC 2 Type II target by Month 12; pre-SOC 2 security documentation available
- **MSA:** Standard Master Service Agreement; enterprise-friendly terms (no auto-renewal, 30-day cure period)
- **Liability caps:** Standard SaaS (1x annual contract value)
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime commitment at Enterprise tier with financial remedy
