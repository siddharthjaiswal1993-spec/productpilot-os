# Positioning

## Positioning Statement (Geoffrey Moore Format)

**For** product teams at B2B SaaS companies who are drowning in disconnected customer, engineering, and business signals,

**ProductPilot OS** is a Product Intelligence OS

**That** transforms fragmented organizational signals into evidence-backed product decisions, cited PRDs, scored prioritization recommendations, and personalized stakeholder alignment communications — with full source attribution and human-in-the-loop governance.

**Unlike** roadmap tools (Productboard, Aha), project management systems (Jira), documentation tools (Notion, Confluence), or enterprise search (Glean),

**ProductPilot OS** is the intelligence layer that sits above all existing PM tools, reasons across their data simultaneously, and generates the evidence foundation that makes every product decision accountable, traceable, and trustworthy.

---

## Category: Product Intelligence OS

The category is the most important positioning decision. We are not a better roadmap tool. We are not an AI writing assistant for PMs. We are not enterprise search for product teams. We are a new category: **Product Intelligence OS** — the intelligence layer for product organizations.

The category name signals:
- **Product:** domain-specific, not generic enterprise AI
- **Intelligence:** reasoning and synthesis, not storage or search
- **OS:** infrastructure layer, not application; the system that product decisions run on

Category positioning means the competitive frame is not "which PM tool is better?" — it is "does your organization need an intelligence layer?" The latter question has a different buyer, a different budget, and a different sales motion.

---

## Target Segment

**Primary:** B2B SaaS product teams, Series B–D, 50–500 employees, 3–20 PMs, $10M–$150M ARR.

**Characteristics:**
- Enterprise customer motion (complex ICP, long sales cycles, high-stakes feature decisions)
- Tool sprawl: 6+ PM-adjacent tools active simultaneously
- Growth pressure: scaling PM team without equivalent revenue growth
- Evidence gap: roadmap decisions frequently challenged by engineering, sales, or executives for lack of evidence backing

**Secondary (Year 2+):** Enterprise product organizations, 500+ employees, 20+ PMs, multiple product lines.

## Frame of Reference

ProductPilot OS sits in a specific architectural position:

```
[Board / Investors]
[CEO / Executive Team]
[CPO / VP Product]          ← ProductPilot OS serves here
[Product Teams + PMs]       ← ProductPilot OS works here
[Engineering / Design / GTM]
[Systems of Record: Jira, Salesforce, Zendesk, Slack, Gong]
```

ProductPilot OS is not a system of record (bottom). It is not a board-level reporting tool (top). It is the intelligence layer between the product team and its data sources — and between the product team and its stakeholders.

This position is neither below nor above the existing PM tool stack — it is the intelligence layer that sits across all of it.
