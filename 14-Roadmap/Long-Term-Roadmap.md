# Long-Term Roadmap: Year 3–5 Vision

## Strategic North Star

By Year 5, ProductPilot OS is the operating system for product-led organizations — the infrastructure layer through which all product intelligence flows, all product decisions are made, and all PM workflows run. The TAM has expanded from "AI tool for PMs" to "product intelligence infrastructure for product-led businesses."

---

## Year 3 Focus: Intelligence Infrastructure at Scale

### The Product Intelligence API Platform
Year 3 transforms ProductPilot OS from a SaaS application into a platform. Organizations build on top of ProductPilot's intelligence layer rather than just consuming it.

- **Partner integrations:** Deep integrations with Figma (design intelligence), GitHub (engineering velocity intelligence), and Intercom (customer intelligence) create a closed-loop product intelligence ecosystem
- **Marketplace:** Third-party PM tools can surface ProductPilot evidence in their own interfaces via API
- **Custom agent builder:** PM teams can configure custom agents for their specific workflow needs

### International Expansion
US market as proof point → expansion to EU (GDPR-native) and UK.

Year 3 milestones:
- EU commercial availability with EU data residency
- €1M ARR from EU customers
- SOC 2 + ISO 27001 certified

### Vertical Market Penetration
Deep vertical specializations for high-concentration PM verticals:

- **FinTech:** Pre-built compliance signal types, regulatory requirement tracking
- **Health Tech:** HIPAA-native, clinical workflow intelligence
- **B2B Enterprise SaaS:** Pre-built enterprise sales signal templates, multi-product portfolio intelligence

## Year 4 Focus: AI-Native Product Organizations

### Autonomous Product Team Assistance
By Year 4, the system operates as a genuine team member — not just a tool. It attends stand-ups (via meeting intelligence), tracks commitments across all communications, flags when reality deviates from the roadmap, and proactively assembles evidence for upcoming decisions.

**Target capability:** A PM at a 500-person company spends <2 hours per week on synthesis tasks (signal review, brief creation, stakeholder updates) — down from 15–20 hours today.

### Predictive Product Intelligence
With 3+ years of outcome data in the knowledge graph, ProductPilot's predictive models achieve genuine accuracy:

- "Features addressing this type of customer need have a 78% probability of achieving >80% adoption at 3 months"
- "This prioritization profile historically correlates with high NPS impact but low revenue impact — is that the right tradeoff for this quarter?"

### Cross-Organization Benchmarking (Anonymous)
Aggregate, anonymized patterns across the ProductPilot OS customer base enable benchmarking:

- "Your evidence-backing rate is in the 60th percentile for B2B SaaS companies of your size"
- "Your PM acceptance rate dropped after a team expansion — this is a common pattern; here are how other teams addressed it"
