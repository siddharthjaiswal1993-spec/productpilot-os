# Messaging Framework

## Core Message Architecture

### Headline (10 words or fewer)
**"The intelligence layer for evidence-backed product decisions."**

### One-Line Description
ProductPilot OS is an AI-native product intelligence platform that synthesizes signals from Slack, Jira, Zendesk, Gong, and Salesforce into cited opportunity briefs, prioritization decisions, and PRDs — so PMs spend their time deciding, not researching.

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"The average PM drowns in 12 disconnected signal sources — Slack, Zendesk, Gong, Jira, Salesforce — but still makes prioritization decisions based on whoever spoke loudest in the last meeting.

ProductPilot OS is the intelligence layer that synthesizes all of those signals automatically, generates evidence-backed opportunity briefs with source citations, scores them with RICE backed by actual evidence, and drafts stakeholder communications personalized per audience — all with human approval required before anything happens.

In the time it takes a PM to find three relevant Zendesk tickets, ProductPilot has already read 6 sources, generated a brief, and is waiting for PM approval."

## Audience-Specific Messaging

### For IC PMs (Users)

**Pain they feel:** "I spend my Sundays trying to synthesize feedback so I can defend my priorities on Monday. The roadmap meeting is in 4 hours and I still don't have evidence for why X should be P1."

**Message:** "ProductPilot generates your evidence brief in 90 seconds. Every claim cites the actual Zendesk ticket, Gong call, or Salesforce note that motivated it. You spend your time deciding, not researching."

**Proof point:** "Alex Chen (IC PM at a 200-person SaaS company) went from 3 hours of Monday prep to 8 minutes. The brief had 6 evidence sources she could click and verify."

---

### For VP Product (Champions)

**Pain they feel:** "My PMs spend 40% of their time doing synthesis work instead of product work. And when I ask why something is P1, I get 'because sales asked for it' — not a business case."

**Message:** "ProductPilot gives you visibility into the quality of your team's decision-making. You see which decisions have strong evidence, which don't, and where the team is flying blind. And your PMs get 8–10 hours per week back for actual product work."

**Proof point:** "VP Product at a 500-person FinTech company reduced roadmap churn by 35% in Q2. Evidence-backed decisions held up in stakeholder meetings; evidence-thin decisions got replaced before they made it to engineering."

---

### For CPO (Economic Buyer)

**Pain they feel:** "I need to know that our product organization is making decisions for the right reasons, not because of who has the best relationship with sales."

**Message:** "ProductPilot creates an immutable audit trail for every product decision your organization makes. Every priority, every brief, every PRD — you can trace the evidence back to the original source. Product decisions become auditable, defensible, and improvable."

**Proof point:** "The Evidence-Backing Rate metric tells you what percentage of roadmap decisions were backed by cited customer, technical, and business evidence. That number should be on your board slide."

## Messaging by Use Case

### Use Case: "I need to justify a priority decision to my VP"

**Message:** "Stop defending opinions. Start presenting evidence. ProductPilot generates a brief with the exact sources that support your prioritization — so your VP is reading the same customer feedback you are."

### Use Case: "I spend too long on PRDs"

**Message:** "PRD first draft in 3 minutes. Every user story has citations. Every edge case is sourced from historical features. You edit, not start from scratch."

### Use Case: "Stakeholder updates take my whole Friday"

**Message:** "One approved decision → 5 personalized stakeholder drafts in 90 seconds. Engineering gets technical scope. Sales gets pipeline context. VP gets business impact. All from the same evidence base."

### Use Case: "We miss customer risk signals until accounts churn"

**Message:** "Risk Watchdog runs every 4 hours. When support ticket volume for 'payment failures' spikes 62% vs. baseline, you get a risk brief with the affected accounts, their ARR, and 3 mitigation options — before you would have manually noticed the pattern."

## Anti-Patterns in Messaging (What NOT to Say)

| Don't Say | Why Not |
|-----------|---------|
| "AI roadmap tool" | Too generic; everything is "AI" now |
| "Chat with your data" | This is a copilot metaphor; ProductPilot is an intelligence layer with agents, not a chat interface |
| "Automates your PM workflow" | Implies replacement; PMs are still in control and making decisions |
| "Never miss a signal" | Overpromise; the system has SLAs, not perfection |
| "Makes decisions for you" | Directly contradicts HITL design; will create trust concerns |
| "Replace your PM tools" | We complement existing tools; not a replacement |

## Vocabulary We Own

These are the terms ProductPilot OS should own in the product management space:

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Signal Collapse** | The failure mode where PM teams have too many disconnected signals to synthesize — so they collapse to gut decisions |
| **Product Intelligence OS** | The category ProductPilot OS is creating: an intelligence layer above PM tools that synthesizes signals into decisions |
| **Evidence-Backing Rate** | % of roadmap decisions backed by cited customer, technical, and business evidence |
| **PM Acceptance Rate** | % of AI-generated outputs approved without major edit — the primary AI quality metric |
| **Confidence Score** | 0–100 composite score measuring evidence quality (volume, diversity, recency, business impact, representativeness) |
| **Decision Debt** | The accumulating cost of undocumented, unevidenced product decisions that must be re-litigated when context is lost |
