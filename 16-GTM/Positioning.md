# Positioning

## Positioning Statement (Geoffrey Moore Format)

**For** product managers and product teams at B2B SaaS companies  
**Who** struggle to synthesize fragmented signals from 12+ sources into evidence-backed decisions  
**ProductPilot OS** is a Product Intelligence Operating System  
**That** automatically synthesizes signals, generates cited opportunity briefs, and provides HITL-gated AI agents for prioritization, PRD generation, risk monitoring, and stakeholder alignment  
**Unlike** roadmap tools (Productboard), project management tools (Jira), or generic AI assistants (ChatGPT)  
**ProductPilot OS** creates an evidence layer above all PM tools: every decision is traceable, every recommendation is cited, and every PM stays in control

---

## Category Position

**Category we're creating:** Product Intelligence OS

**What this category is NOT:**
- Not a roadmap tool (we don't manage the roadmap)
- Not a project management tool (we don't manage tickets)
- Not a feedback inbox (we synthesize, we don't just collect)
- Not a chatbot ("chat with your data" is not the right metaphor)
- Not a note-taking tool (we generate structured intelligence, not notes)

**What this category IS:**
The intelligence layer that sits above systems of record. It reads what's in your Slack, Jira, Zendesk, Gong, and Salesforce — and synthesizes it into the product intelligence that PMs need to make good decisions.

## Positioning by Buyer Role

### IC PM
**Position:** "ProductPilot is your evidence engine. You still make the decisions — it just makes sure every decision you make is backed by something you can show to your VP."

**Contrast with status quo:** "You go from spending Sunday researching to generating a brief on Monday morning that would have taken you days."

### VP Product
**Position:** "ProductPilot is your decision quality layer. You see exactly how your team is making decisions, what evidence supports them, and where the gaps are. Your team spends more time on product work and less on synthesis."

**Contrast with status quo:** "You stop getting 'sales asked for it' as a justification. You start seeing evidence-backed cases that hold up in stakeholder meetings."

### CPO
**Position:** "ProductPilot is your organizational intelligence system. Product decisions are auditable, evidence-backed, and traceable back to customer voice. Your board can see what you built and why."

**Contrast with status quo:** "You stop losing institutional knowledge when PMs leave. The decision record is in the system."

## The Analogy That Explains the Category

**Gong** is for revenue teams what **ProductPilot OS** is for product teams.

Gong captures every sales conversation, analyzes it for revenue intelligence, and helps sales leaders understand what's working and what's not — with citations to actual calls. Before Gong, sales leaders relied on rep self-reporting and intuition. After Gong, they have evidence.

ProductPilot OS does the same for product decisions. Before ProductPilot, PMs relied on whoever spoke loudest and their own memory of scattered conversations. After ProductPilot, they have evidence — cited, structured, and traceable.

Gong reached $250M ARR because revenue intelligence was genuinely missing from the revenue tech stack. Product intelligence is genuinely missing from the product tech stack.
