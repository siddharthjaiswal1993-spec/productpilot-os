# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

ProductPilot OS is priced to match the value it delivers: PM time savings, better decision quality, and reduced decision risk. The pricing reflects the outcome value, not the feature list.

A PM saving 10 hours/week at a fully-loaded cost of $150K/year saves ~$750 in labor value per week. At $49/month, the ROI math is overwhelmingly favorable — and that's before counting the revenue impact of better prioritization decisions.

## Tier Structure

**Billed annually. Minimum: 2 seats. Maximum: 10 seats.**

**What's included:**
- Signal Intelligence Hub (up to 5 integrations)
- Insight Synthesizer Agent (opportunity briefs)
- Prioritization Agent (RICE scoring)
- Risk Watchdog Agent (monitoring alerts)
- Knowledge Graph (basic entity model)
- HITL approval workflows
- Audit log (1-year retention)
- Signal ingestion: up to 1,000 signals/month

**Best for:** 2–5 PM teams at Series A–B companies, first adopters, PLG entry

**Example:** 3-PM team at a 150-person SaaS company → $147/month ($1,764/year)

## Pricing Rationale

### Why $49 for Starter?
- Low enough to be an individual PM expense or a no-deliberation team purchase
- High enough to signal professional-grade tooling (not a free tier)
- Creates a natural expansion motion: teams that start at Starter quickly need Team features (PRD Copilot, Stakeholder Alignment)

### Why $99 for Team?
- 2x Starter reflects the step-up in value: PRD Copilot + Meeting Intelligence + Stakeholder Alignment are each significant time-savers
- Benchmarks against comparable tools: Notion ($16/seat), Productboard ($80/seat), Gong ($1,200/seat/year → $100/seat/month)
- A VP Product approves $99/seat without significant deliberation for a product their team will use daily

### Why Annual Billing Only?
- V1 launches with annual billing only to manage cash flow and reduce churn risk
- Monthly billing may be offered in V2 for Starter tier at a 20% premium

## Expansion Mechanics

### Seat Expansion
As PM teams grow, seats expand automatically. ProductPilot OS pricing grows with the team.

**Expansion triggers:**
- Team hires 2+ new PMs → champion adds seats
- VP Product sees value → expands from 3-seat Starter to 8-seat Team
- CPO sees ROI → company-wide Enterprise rollout

**Target NRR:** >120% (expansion revenue exceeds churn in existing accounts)

### Tier Expansion
Team customers upgrade to Enterprise when:
- PM team >20 seats (exceeds Team maximum)
- Security requirements trigger (SSO, SOC 2, compliance)
- CPO/CTO gets involved and requires enterprise contract terms
