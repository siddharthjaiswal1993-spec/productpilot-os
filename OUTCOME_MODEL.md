# Outcome Model — ProductPilot OS

---

## The Value Hypothesis

ProductPilot OS creates value by reducing the time PMs spend assembling context and by improving the quality of the decisions made with that context. Better product decisions — more aligned with customer evidence, more aware of risk, more traceable to outcomes — produce better product outcomes: higher retention, faster roadmap velocity, fewer wasted quarters.

---

## Business Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Net Revenue Retention | >130% | Expansion across modules (PRD → Signal → Roadmap → Executive) drives NRR |
| Time to first value | <7 days (first evidence-backed output generated) | Short activation time drives trial-to-paid conversion |
| Module adoption per account | >3 active modules | Cross-module adoption indicates platform dependency |
| ACV | $24K–$120K depending on team size | Reflects platform vs. point-tool pricing |

---

## Product Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Citation accuracy | >95% (cited source contains claimed content) | Trust depends on verifiable citations |
| Recommendation acceptance rate | >60% | Accepted recommendations signal the platform is useful |
| Evidence brief completeness | 100% (all required fields populated) | Incomplete outputs drive users back to manual methods |
| Knowledge graph freshness | <2-hour staleness for webhook-connected sources | Stale context produces irrelevant outputs |

---

## AI Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Hallucination rate | <2% (verified against source documents) | Any hallucination in a PM decision context destroys trust |
| Cross-signal recall | >85% on golden dataset | Multi-source synthesis is the core value; recall must be high |
| Opportunity brief precision | >70% (rated useful by PM reviewer) | Precision on generated briefs measures real output quality |
| Trust score per agent | Composite; advancing toward L3 autonomy in 90 days | Autonomy advancement is the long-term engagement driver |

---

## The Metric That Matters Most

**Evidence brief acceptance rate.**

If PMs review an AI-generated opportunity brief and proceed to use it — editing and submitting it, rather than discarding it and starting fresh — the platform is producing usable outputs. That metric, above all others, measures whether the intelligence layer is trustworthy enough to change how PMs work.
